**# Problem Statement- CalorieTracker App**







**## Problem**

*People are unable to measure their calorie intake efficiently because:-*

* *Existing Apps are too hard to use*
* *Too many unnecessary featured that is not needed*
* *hard to use on a daily basis*
* 
*&nbsp;*



**## Solution**

* *Let users input their own daily calorie goals*
* *Getting food options aligned to their input*
* *Daily output on progress*
* *A simple but welcoming interface*







**## Target Audience**

* *Amateurs in calorie dieting*
* *People who want a simple experience and no complexity*
* *Those who only want output based on calories and no other complex features*







**## Success Criteria**

* Users can easily add foods and see their daily total
* The app accurately calculates remaining calories
* Interface is  straightforward  and requires no instructions
